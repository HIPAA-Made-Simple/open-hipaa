Yes, if your policies permit it and the device is safeguarded and tracked in your inventory. Encryption is an addressable HIPAA specification that is strongly expected for portable devices, so before a laptop or drive with PHI leaves the facility you should encrypt it (or implement and document an equivalent safeguard), since lost unencrypted devices are a leading cause of reportable breaches.
