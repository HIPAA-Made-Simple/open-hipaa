Often yes, on pages patients log into. Tracking scripts like the Meta Pixel or Google Analytics can send a vendor that has signed no business associate agreement identifiable information about what a patient views or books, which is an impermissible disclosure, so keep such trackers off pages where patients authenticate or manage their care.
