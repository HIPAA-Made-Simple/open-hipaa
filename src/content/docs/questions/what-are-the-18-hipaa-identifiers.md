They are names; geographic units smaller than a state; all date elements except year; phone numbers; fax numbers; email addresses; Social Security numbers; medical record numbers; health plan beneficiary numbers; account numbers; certificate or license numbers; vehicle identifiers; device identifiers; URLs; IP addresses; biometric identifiers; full-face photos; and any other unique identifying number, characteristic, or code. Under Safe Harbor, all 18 must be removed to de-identify data.
