Usually yes. A misdirected email, fax, or letter containing PHI is presumed to be a reportable breach, and you must notify unless a four-factor risk assessment shows a low probability the PHI was compromised or a narrow exception applies (such as a good-faith belief the unintended recipient could not reasonably have retained the information).
