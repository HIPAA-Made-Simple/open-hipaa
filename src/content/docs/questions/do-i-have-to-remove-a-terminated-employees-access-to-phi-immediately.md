In practice, yes. HIPAA's Security Rule lists termination procedures as an addressable specification, so you must either implement a process to revoke a departing or reassigned workforce member's access to ePHI or document why not and adopt a reasonable alternative; access should be revoked promptly, ideally the moment the departure takes effect, to prevent misuse.
