Immediately for large breaches, annually for small ones: a breach affecting 500 or more individuals must be reported to HHS without unreasonable delay and no later than 60 days after discovery, while breaches affecting fewer than 500 may be logged and reported to HHS annually, no later than 60 days after the end of the calendar year in which they were discovered.
