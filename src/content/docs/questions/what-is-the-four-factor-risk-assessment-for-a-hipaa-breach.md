It is the analysis used to decide whether an impermissible use or disclosure must be reported, weighing (1) the nature and extent of the PHI, (2) who used or received it, (3) whether it was actually acquired or viewed, and (4) how well the risk was mitigated. If these factors show a low probability the PHI was compromised, no notification is required.
