No, not into consumer AI tools. Entering identifiable patient information into a public chatbot like ChatGPT discloses PHI to a vendor that has signed no business associate agreement, which HIPAA does not permit; PHI may be used only on an enterprise or API tier that executes a BAA and is configured not to train on your data.
