Generally yes. Keeping a record of the hardware and media that store PHI and their movements is an addressable HIPAA implementation specification—meaning you must either maintain such a device inventory or document and implement a reasonable alternative—so an up-to-date inventory of computers, servers, laptops, and drives is the expected practice.
