It depends on the tier of culpability. HIPAA sets civil penalties in four tiers, from a violation you did not know about up to uncorrected willful neglect, each with its own per-violation minimum and an annual cap for identical violations; the exact dollar figures adjust for inflation every year, so confirm them in the current HHS penalty schedule.
