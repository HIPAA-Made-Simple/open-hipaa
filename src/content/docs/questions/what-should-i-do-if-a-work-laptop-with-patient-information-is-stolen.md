Report it to your privacy or security officer right away and investigate. If the laptop's drive was encrypted to HHS-recognized standards the PHI is 'secured' and it generally isn't a reportable breach; if it wasn't, you must run a breach risk assessment and likely notify the affected patients and HHS.
