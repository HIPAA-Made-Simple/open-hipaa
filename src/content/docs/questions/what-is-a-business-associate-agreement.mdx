A Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is the contract HIPAA requires between a
covered entity (like a medical or therapy practice) and any vendor — a
"business associate" — that creates, receives, maintains, or transmits
protected health information on the practice's behalf. Signing it makes the
vendor directly accountable under HIPAA for safeguarding that data.
