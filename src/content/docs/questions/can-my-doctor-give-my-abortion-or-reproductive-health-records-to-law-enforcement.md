Not without a valid legal basis. HIPAA never requires a provider to disclose records to law enforcement and permits it only in narrow circumstances such as a court order, warrant, or grand jury subpoena; because federal reproductive-health privacy protections have been in flux, confirm current HHS rules before disclosing.
